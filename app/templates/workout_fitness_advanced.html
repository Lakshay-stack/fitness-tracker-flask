🏋️‍♀️ Advanced Fitness Workout Plan

A 5-day high-performance workout plan combining cardio, strength, and agility.

Day 1: Power & Plyometrics

  • Box Jumps – 4 sets x 8
  • Burpees – 3 sets x 15
  • Kettlebell Swings – 3 sets x 20

Day 2: Upper Body + HIIT

  • Pull-ups – 4 sets to failure
  • DB Chest Press – 4 sets x 10
  • 20-min HIIT Circuit

Day 3: Lower Body + Core

  • Back Squats – 5 sets x 6
  • Leg Press – 4 sets x 10
  • Plank Variations – 4 sets x 1 min

Day 4: Active Recovery

  • Mobility Stretching – 30 minutes
  • Foam Rolling – 15 minutes
  • Swimming or Yoga – 30 minutes

Day 5: Full Body Burn

  • Thrusters – 4 sets x 12
  • Renegade Rows – 3 sets x 10
  • Rowing Machine – 15 minutes
