🏆 Advanced Workout Plan for Building Muscle

This 6-day intense training split uses progressive overload, advanced techniques like supersets, and heavy compound lifts to maximize hypertrophy.

Day 1: Chest & Triceps

  • Barbell Bench Press – 5 sets x 5 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 4 sets x 8 reps
  • Cable Fly Superset with Push-ups – 3 sets
  • Close-grip Bench Press – 4 sets x 10 reps

Day 2: Back & Biceps

  • Deadlifts – 5 sets x 5 reps
  • Weighted Pull-ups – 4 sets
  • T-Bar Rows – 4 sets x 10 reps
  • Barbell Curls Superset with Hammer Curls – 3 sets

Day 3: Legs

  • Back Squats – 5 sets x 6 reps
  • Bulgarian Split Squats – 4 sets x 8 reps
  • Romanian Deadlifts – 4 sets x 10 reps
  • Leg Curls Superset with Calf Raises – 3 sets

Day 4: Shoulders

  • Military Press – 5 sets x 6 reps
  • Lateral Raises – 4 sets x 15 reps
  • Rear Delt Fly – 4 sets x 12 reps
  • Arnold Press Superset with Shrugs – 3 sets

Day 5: Arms Focus

  • EZ Bar Curls – 4 sets x 12 reps
  • Preacher Curls – 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Triceps Rope Pushdown – 4 sets x 12 reps
  • Overhead Dumbbell Extensions – 3 sets x 10 reps

Day 6: Rest or Active Recovery

Optional: Light cardio, yoga, or mobility work.
