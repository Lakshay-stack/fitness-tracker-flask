🏃‍♂️ Beginner Fitness Workout Plan

A gentle introduction to physical fitness. Focused on full-body movements and building consistency.

Day 1: Cardio + Core

  • Brisk Walk – 30 minutes
  • Bodyweight Squats – 3 sets x 15
  • Crunches – 3 sets x 20

Day 2: Upper Body

  • Wall Push-ups – 3 sets x 15
  • Seated Dumbbell Press – 3 sets x 12
  • Arm Circles – 2 sets x 30 sec

Day 3: Lower Body + Balance

  • Bodyweight Lunges – 3 sets x 10/leg
  • Glute Bridges – 3 sets x 12
  • Single Leg Stands – 3 sets x 20 sec
